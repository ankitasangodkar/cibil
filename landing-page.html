Myths and Facts

There are many credit myths in the financial world. Here are a few around CIBIL Score.
Myth
My CIBIL Score depends on my income and/or bank savings.
Facts
No, it does not. Your CIBIL Score is based on your loan repayment behavior, length of credit history, credit mix and utilization.
Myth
I cannot bounce back from a low CIBIL Score.
Facts
You definitely can! A good credit behavior, positive repayment habits and a credit-conscious effort will help you rebuild your score.

What is a CIBIL Score and what factors affect my CIBIL Score?

CIBIL Score is a 3 digit numeric summary of your credit history, derived by using details found in the 'Accounts' and 'Enquiries' sections on your CIBIL Report and ranges from 300 to 900. The closer your score is to 900, the higher are the chances of your loan application getting approved.
